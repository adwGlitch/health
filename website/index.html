How FarmGuard AI Works

A seamless pipeline from raw sensor data to actionable health intelligence.

01

Sensors Collect Data

ESP32 microcontroller reads data from DHT22 (temperature), MPU6050 (motion), MQ2 (gas), and potentiometer (heart rate simulation) in real time.

02

AI Analysis Engine

The FarmGuard AI engine ingests multi-sensor data streams and runs intelligent algorithms to detect patterns, anomalies, and health deviations instantly.

03

Health Assessment

A composite health score (0–100) is computed from all parameters. The AI generates natural-language risk assessments and identifies possible causes for anomalies.

04

Alerts & Actions

Smart alerts fire immediately when thresholds are crossed. The Recommendation Agent provides veterinary guidance and preventive actions to protect the herd.
